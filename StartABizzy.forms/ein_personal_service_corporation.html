What Is an EIN/Tax ID?

An EIN/Tax ID is a number assigned by the Internal Revenue Service to identify a business for tax purposes. You must include your business's tax ID on bank account applications, income tax forms, and employment tax reports and payments.

A EIN/Tax ID for a business serves the same purposes as a Social Security number for individuals.
